Fund Raiser'23

'Fundraiser's 2023, Let's Do Our Bit' is an annual fundraising initiative 
organized 
by the political science department. The event showcases an 
esteemed lineup of artists rom different regions of India, ranging from 
the deserts of Rajasthan to the Arabian sea. The initiative aims 
to raise funds for social welfare causes throughart and music. 
The artists perform a range of traditional and contemporary forms of 
art, including folk music, classical dance, and street plays. 
 The event provides a platform for artists to showcase their talent 
 and contribute to a noble cause. The initiative has been a huge 
 success in the past,and the political science department looks 
 forward to continued support from the community for this noble cause.
