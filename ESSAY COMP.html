Essay Competition

The National Essay Competition, organized by the Xavier's Political 
Science Association, is a highly anticipated event that takes place 
every year. The competition is presented by Parimal K Shroff, an 
esteemed alumnus of the college, and offers a platform for students 
across the country to showcase their writing skills and critical 
thinking. Participants are invited to submit essays on a given topic 
related to politics, governance, and social issues. The competition 
is open to students from different academic backgrounds and offers 
an opportunity to win attractive cash prizes and gain recognition. 
It's a great way for young minds to engage in intellectual discourse 
and contribute to the nation's socio-political fabric.
